Project Overview

A fictional brand was created from two scrabble tiles and one playing card drawn at random. Through several iterations, Lazy Zebra as a pajama company was developed from the given combination: L, Z, and 2. Once conceptualized, the challenge of designing a cohesive branding identity and corresponding animation presented itself.

Storyboarding

Initial ideas were sketched and two selected concepts were expanded upon in the form of frame by frame storyboarding. The concept that communicated the overall mood of the brand the best was selected to be produced digitally.

Digital Storyboarding

Pencil sketches were drawn and refined in Adobe Illustrator. These vectors were imported into Adobe AfterEffects for further manipulation.

Putting into Motion

Adobe AfterEffects was the main tool for animation production. Several layers of small components were manipulated in order to form a simple character. The final touches including main and background audio were added to bring it to life.
