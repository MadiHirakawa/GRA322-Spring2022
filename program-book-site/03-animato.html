An engaging way to introduce children to music

App Design

Project Overview

This mobile application was designed to provide a fun and engaging way to teach young children the fundamentals of music. The application considers the parent as well as the child in its approach to its design.

Research & Wireframes

Reasearch on existing mobile applications for children's music education was conducted. Components such as educational features and general organization of information were considered. Several wireframe and user flow sketches were drafted in order to craft a realistic experience.

Designing Characters

Characters for the minigames were illustrated first on paper and then refined in Adobe Illustrator.

Crafting the Interface

The purpose of the application was drafted. A light and playful color palette was applied in addition to a friendly typeface.
